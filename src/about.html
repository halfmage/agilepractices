About me

I am originally from Minsk, Belarus. Had a chance to start my IT-career there and kick-start my IT-path. In 2014, I have moved to Germany and did my Master in IT-Project Management and continued working in Trivago and later at Vodafone.

My main goal is dedication to my work and experience. Communication with partners, new solutions and creativity in working process, flexible attitude in each case – this is what gives me strength each day.

Let’s build a solid Agile community together and learn from each other!
